Quads & Choppers/BOUND @ LHC

August 14, 2016

Race event:

BOUND is a friendly club-level race meeting run in the midlands several times a year, and was on this occasion hosted at Leamington Hobby Centre, near Pillerton Priors in Warwickshire.
Check out the Facebook group here.

Format:

24 pilots competed in four heats of six.
Five rounds of qualifying, finals-for-all.
Races over 2 minutes, no race-distance limit.
Competitors are ranked after each round by number of laps completed and time taken, and round-by-round (RBR) scoring awards points based on ranking.
Overall qualifying positions are calculated from each competitor's best three scores.
Every competitor flies in a Final.
